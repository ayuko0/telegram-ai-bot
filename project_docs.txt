Giants Protocol is the retail intelligence and trading platform operated by 2MR Labs.

2MR Labs is a digital assets and tokenization company focused on real-world assets (RWA), institutional infrastructure, and retail market intelligence.

Giants Protocol operates through giantsprotocol.ai, an AI-powered crypto investment, trading, and market analysis platform.

Core Functions of 2MR Labs:
- Onboarding real-world assets (RWA) onto blockchain infrastructure.
- Structuring assets for global institutional and sovereign investors.
- Powering retail crypto markets through AI-driven analysis and trading via giantsprotocol.ai.
- Distributing assets through a tokenized financial stack.

Giants Protocol Role:
Giants Protocol serves as the retail intelligence and distribution layer within the 2MR Labs ecosystem. It enables retail user growth, sentiment analysis, and capital flow tracking through AI-driven tools.

Key Capabilities of giantsprotocol.ai:
- AI-powered crypto trading and market analysis.
- Retail sentiment and behavioral data collection.
- Analysis of capital flows and market demand.
- Supporting data-driven de-risking of tokenized real-world assets.

Retail Intelligence:
Giants Protocol gathers large-scale retail behavior and sentiment data through its platform. This data is used internally to inform asset structuring and demand validation for tokenized assets launched by 2MR Labs.

Assets Tokenized Through the 2MR Labs Platform:
- Luxury real estate (including properties in Dubai, Tokyo, and Singapore).
- Private credit funds.
- Data centers and AI compute infrastructure.
- Carbon credits.
- Bitcoin and digital assets.
- Art, intellectual property, and gaming assets.

Services Offered by 2MR Labs:
- RWA Tokenization as a Service (TaaS): Infrastructure for converting physical and financial assets into on-chain digital assets.
- Digital Asset and Blockchain Consulting: Strategic advisory services for institutions, governments, and sovereign entities.
- giantsprotocol.ai SaaS Platform: AI-powered retail trading, analytics, and market intelligence.
- Asset distribution through institutional and retail networks.

Distribution Network:
2MR Labs distributes assets through a private network that includes institutional investors, family offices, large capital allocators, and retail users using giantsprotocol.ai.

Company Operations:
2MR Labs operates with a lean team supported by AI agents and on-chain dashboards. Human operators oversee asset structuring, engineering, analytics, and distribution.

Team Roles:
- Deal Architects: Source real-world asset issuers.
- Token Engineers: Launch and manage tokenized assets.
- AI and Data Scientists: Optimize giantsprotocol.ai algorithms and data systems.
- Distribution Leads: Manage institutional and large-capital asset distribution.
- AI Agents: Handle compliance workflows, analytics, and continuous retail support.

Revenue Sources:
- Tokenization fees.
- Asset servicing fees.
- giantsprotocol.ai SaaS and trading-related revenue.
- Digital asset and blockchain consulting services.

Company Positioning:
2MR Labs is positioned as a digital asset infrastructure and intelligence provider operating across institutional and retail markets.

Identity:
2MR Labs functions as a global financial operating system integrating asset tokenization, AI-driven retail intelligence, and institutional distribution through Giants Protocol.
